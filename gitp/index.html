Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an appointment online by filling out the form above. Select your preferred doctor and time.

Please bring a valid ID and your medical history (if any). Your doctor will guide you through the process.

Consultations typically last 30 to 45 minutes, depending on your needs and the doctor's specialty.
